## ✨ Features : 
- **🔄 No Empty Input:** The Input box doesnt accept any empty imputs.
- **🔄 Counting:** Your Tasks will be numbered .
- **🔄 Priority-Selector :** You can add Prioity on your task (it lets you know where to focus first) by tagging your task with  'LOW', 'MEDIUM' or 'HIGH' with colors =).
- **🔄 Change Task Status Anytime:** You can easily change the status of your Task from Ongoing to Done and Vice versa at any moment you feel like changing.
- **💾 Locally Storing your Tasks:** Your tasks are added in the loacal storage so that it doesn't erase your task everytime you refresh the site or even close the browser.
